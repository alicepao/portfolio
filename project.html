My Data Science Project Process

I love working on data science projects. They allow me to apply what I've learned in classes to solve problems in real life. Most of my projects focus on R, SQL, and Power Bi applications. Here are the steps I take to complete a data science project:

If I come across any interesting data sets or topics I'd like to explore more, I start to plan for a potential personal project. Kaggle is my favorite place to find data resources or personal project inspirations.

Data exploratory, data transformation and data cleaning take up to 75% of the toal project hours. Model/Algorithm buidling takes 15%, and the rest of time is spent on trouble shooting or writing summary reports or presentations.
